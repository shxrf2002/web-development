Incredible Sense of Smell: Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell,estimated to be 100,000 times better than humans. They possess up to 300 million olfactory receptors in their noses,allowing them to detect scents and pheromones that are undetectable to us.

Wet Noses: A dog's nose is wet to help absorb scent particles. This moisture enhances their ability to smell, making their noses not just cute but also functional.
